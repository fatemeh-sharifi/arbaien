Have you ever wanted to pack your things and live on an island alone? Sometimes we want to run away from everyone and everything. Then we think to ourselves that real life is not definitely like Dr. Ernst's family or Robinson Crusoe's story, where it is easy to live alone.
When we think and review our needs, we realize that we can't live alone. That's right! Because human being is sociable and has been created for social life.
Mankind lives among people because of the relationship he has with people in the society and his needs, and one of the main goals is that man can develop and grow in doing good.
This is why seclusion, going to a corner and cutting off communication with people is forbidden, because when a person is in a group, he is exposed to sacrifice, kindness, generosity, backbiting, lies and etc., otherwise, it is much easier to live alone.
social life can harm a person at the same time if it is not planned, from the influence of bad friends to the media, bad news and lies to many other things.
One of the most important damages that manifests itself in social life is that it makes problems and challenges in prioritizing activities. For example, you were going to prepare for saying prayer on time, but you saw a message or an interesting article on your phone or you went to do something for two minutes. The name of this damage in the Quran is "obsession"; (vasvas khanas) which creates doubt on what you know and want to do, just like obsession, you have probably seen someone who is obsessed.
